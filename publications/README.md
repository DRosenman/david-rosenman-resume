\### \*\*Another Useful Application Developed Using R (2018)\*\*  

\*\*Author\*\*, with Joseph Aiello  

\*National Water Monitoring News\*, National Water Quality Monitoring Council



Article describing the development of an R-based analytical application used by NJ DEP to support watershed-scale water quality analysis, including nitrate investigations in the Passaic River basin. The application integrated live data from national water-quality databases, spatial analysis, and interactive visualizations to support regulatory assessment and source-tracking efforts.



\- \*\*Year:\*\* 2018  

\- \*\*Publication:\*\* \*National Water Monitoring News\* (Fall 2018)  

\- \*\*Context:\*\* NJ DEP internal and interagency analytical tooling
